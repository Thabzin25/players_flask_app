Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our football scouting system.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. If you continue to have issues, contact our support team.

How can I add new players to the system?

To add a new player, navigate to the Players section and click the "Add New Player" button. Fill in the required information and save. Administrators can bulk upload players using the import tool in the admin panel.

What metrics are used to evaluate players?

Our system uses over 100 different metrics including technical skills (passing accuracy, shot accuracy), physical attributes (speed, stamina), tactical awareness (positioning, decision making), and mental attributes (leadership, resilience). You can customize which metrics are prioritized in your evaluations.

How does the live chat support work?

Our live chat connects you directly with our support team in real-time. Simply type your question in the chat window and one of our support specialists will assist you. The chat system also includes automated responses for common questions for instant help.

Can I export data from the system?

Yes, you can export data in various formats including CSV, PDF, and Excel. Navigate to the Reports section to generate and export customized reports. Administrative users have additional export options available.
